ABOUT OUR SITE


This website is created keeping in mind the hurdles faced by many people, especially students. There are many instances where we postpone or cancel a trip as the overall expenses go above budget, especially the cost of the commute. Through this website, people can book flights at affordable costs and at prices lower than those on other airline websites. Students and the elderly with limited income benefit the most from the coupon codes that are available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.



Florida is known to be a tourist spot, with Miami and Orlando as its hotspots. Considering logistics, we have limited flight services locally to Florida between Tampa, Orlando, Miami, Gainesville, Melbourne, Key West, and Jacksonville. As students, there is a need for us to travel locally for not only academic internships but also to explore different places, make memories, and reminisce about them in the future. This website will help us travel guilt-free. In the future, we want to expand the website to support travel within the USA. This website allows passengers to check in virtually within 24 hours of departure time. Through virtual check-in, passengers can download their boarding passes and enter directly through the boarding gate, hassle-free. This feature eliminates the practice of waiting in lines. The user gets timely updates about the flights. Users can also access information on various policies, FAQs, and baggage allowances.



In addition to the already-affordable fares, students can avail themselves of a special “student privilege” discount by signing up with their corresponding university email. Once you get verified as a student, we guarantee that you can fly with MLVT at much cheaper fares than any other means of transport. Our mission is to provide a seamless flying experience. Hence, we strive to keep our fares as low and transparent as possible—no hidden fees or taxes. Though the fares are cheap, the flight isn’t. We take pride in keeping our flights neat and clean. Every little detail is taken care of with the utmost attention; no compromises are made when it comes to flying clean. Apart from the technical checks, the interior of the plane is sanitized for every trip, and the wear and tear are replaced.



Currently, the website is static in nature. The available flights and fares are displayed on a single page, but in the future, we will include JavaScript to make the website dynamic. We will enable the seat selection option. Based on the inputs from the itinerary search form and the seat selected, a ticket along with the boarding pass will be displayed. Adding to this, we will enable the coupon code application at the time of checkout.
